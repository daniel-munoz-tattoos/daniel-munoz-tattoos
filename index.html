About Daniel

Born and raised in Holland, Michigan, Daniel Munoz brings 4 years of tattooing expertise to the table. Growing up around the Great Lakes, he infuses his art with a laid-back, honest energy. Inspired by the mellow beats of Mac Miller and the authenticity of Midwest life, Daniel works closely with clients—just good conversation, respect, and ink that tells your story.
